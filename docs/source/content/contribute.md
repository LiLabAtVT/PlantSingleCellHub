# Contribute

Thank you for your interest in contributing to PlantSCHub!

PlantSCHub is a community-curated resource that grows through shared databases, protocols, publications, tutorials, and documentation updates. Even small contributions—such as a missing resource, a corrected link, or a clearer description—can help future users.

## Ways to contribute

- **Suggest a new resource:** Help expand PlantSCHub by suggesting databases, publications, protocols, tutorials, videos, or visualization resources.
- **Improve documentation:** Help make the site clearer, easier to navigate, and more useful for new users.
- **Report an issue:** Open an issue for broken links, outdated information, missing content, or formatting problems in the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/LiLabAtVT/PlantSingleCellHub).
- **Submit a pull request:** Submit a pull request to the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/LiLabAtVT/PlantSingleCellHub) with a short explanation of what you changed and why.

## What makes a strong contribution?
A good contribution includes a clear title, a reliable link, and enough context to show why the resource is useful.

- Keep changes focused and easy to review.
- Follow the site’s existing style and format.
- Add brief, practical context when needed.

## Need help getting started?
If you are not sure how to contribute, open an issue describing what you want to add or improve, or email us. Small contributions are welcome.
